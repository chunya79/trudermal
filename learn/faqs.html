Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Trudermal devices, treatment protocols, safety, and results.

About the Devices

What is the Trudermal Glow?
Using the power of LED technology, this medical-grade device helps target spots, blemishes, lines, and wrinkles over the entire face. It's a wearable, hands-free device using 465nm blue, 633nm red, and 850nm near-infrared wavelengths — delivering clinic-quality results in 10 minutes at home.
Is Trudermal TGA and FDA approved?
Yes. Trudermal is TGA approved as a Class IIa medical device, FDA cleared, and conforms to international safety standards. This means it has been independently assessed for both safety and efficacy before being available to consumers.
How many LEDs does the Trudermal Glow have?
The Trudermal Glow has 132 LEDs per treatment — engineered for full facial coverage including the upper lip, which many competitor masks omit.
Does the device use each wavelength independently?
No. Trudermal devices use triple-chip technology, delivering all wavelengths within a given mode simultaneously during each treatment. This maximises the combined therapeutic effect.

Treatment Modes & Protocol

What are the treatment modes?
The Trudermal Glow has 3 mode settings:

Mode 1 — Anti-Acne: Blue (465nm) + Near-infrared (850nm). Targets bacteria and sebaceous gland activity.

Mode 2 — Acne Repair: Blue (465nm) + Red (633nm). Eliminates bacteria while initiating skin rejuvenation.

Mode 3 — Cell Rejuvenation: Red (633nm) + Near-infrared (850nm). Stimulates collagen and elastin for anti-ageing results.
Which treatment mode is best for acne?
Anti-Acne mode (Mode 1) is best for pustular, active acne. Acne Repair mode (Mode 2) is better suited for adult or hormonal acne where some skin rejuvenation is also desired.
Which treatment mode is best for anti-ageing?
Cell Rejuvenation mode (Mode 3) uses red and near-infrared light combined to stimulate collagen and elastin production — the key proteins responsible for skin firmness and smoothness.
Which treatment mode is best for dermatitis or eczema?
Cell Rejuvenation mode (Mode 3) is recommended as it aids skin healing by boosting collagen and elastin production while reducing inflammation. Consult your healthcare provider if you have severe or active eczema.
Can I use different mode settings on the same day?
No. Stick with one mode setting for the full 6-week treatment period before switching. This allows your skin to fully respond to a specific wavelength combination rather than receiving mixed signals.
Are there treatment recommendations?
For best results, treat 3–5 times per week for a minimum of 6 weeks. Maintenance treatment should be carried out as required after your initial programme. Consistency is the most important factor — results build cumulatively.
Can I use Trudermal every day?
No. Use only 3–5 times weekly. There is no evidence that daily use results in more effective outcomes — the skin needs recovery time between sessions to process the cellular signals and produce new collagen.
How long does LED light therapy take to work?
You should start to see results from around 4 weeks of consistent use. Taking before-and-after photos every 2–4 weeks helps you track subtle but real changes in skin quality and tone.

Safety & Contraindications

Are LED light therapy devices safe?
Trudermal devices are TGA approved for safety and effectiveness for all skin types and conform to international safety standards. When used as directed and within the stated contraindications, they are safe for home use.
Are there any contraindications?
Do not use Trudermal devices if you:

• Are pregnant, planning pregnancy, or breastfeeding
• Have Lupus erythematosus, photosensitive eczema, or Albinism
• Have any photosensitive disorder
• Experience light-induced headaches or epilepsy
• Have a genetic eye condition
• Are taking medications causing photosensitivity (certain antibiotics, chemotherapy drugs, diuretics)
• Are exposed to photosensitising substances (St. John's Wort, coal tar, certain soaps)

If in doubt, consult your healthcare provider before use.
Can I use if pregnant or breastfeeding?
No. Trudermal devices have not been tested on pregnant or breastfeeding women and therefore the risk to the foetus, neonate, or pregnant woman is unknown. Do not use during pregnancy or while breastfeeding.
Is Trudermal Glow safe with Retinol or Retin-A?
Prescriptive Retin-A is not recommended for concurrent use. Discontinue Retin-A for 7 days before using Trudermal. For over-the-counter retinol products, check the product label for any light sensitivity warnings before use.
Can I use serums or creams under the device?
No. Use on clean, bare skin only. Applying serums or creams before treatment creates a barrier that reduces the effectiveness of light penetration. Apply your skincare immediately after treatment instead.
Can I use if I have cancer?
Not recommended during active cancer treatment. Many chemotherapy medications cause photosensitivity. Consult your oncologist or healthcare provider before use.
Can I move around while using Trudermal Glow?
Yes. The Glow is completely portable and battery-powered. You can move freely, watch TV, read, or go about your routine during the 10-minute session.

Device Care & Technical

How do I charge the device?
The device charges via micro USB and takes approximately 6 hours to fully charge. Each full charge provides approximately 10 treatment sessions. Battery capacity: 3.7v, 2600mAH, 9.62Wh lithium ion polymer.
How many treatments per charge?
Approximately 10 treatment sessions per full charge.
How do I clean and maintain the device?
Clean the transparent (face-side) of the mask and the rear with a water-based wipe or a damp clean cloth after each use. Store at room temperature in dry conditions. Avoid direct sunlight and high humidity. Do not submerge in water.
What should I do if I experience a technical problem?
First check that the battery is adequately charged and all USB connections are secure. If issues persist within the warranty period, contact us for support. Trudermal devices are covered by a 12-month manufacturer's warranty against manufacturing defects.
What is the warranty policy?
Under normal use, Trudermal products are guaranteed for 12 months after the original purchase date against manufacturing defects. This warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidental damage, or normal wear and tear.
