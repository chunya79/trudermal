Our Story

The Trudermal Difference

Founded by skin experts. Built for everyone who deserves expert-level skincare — every day.

The Founders

Karin & Natasha

Trudermal was established by Karin and Natasha — two skin professionals with over 20 years of combined experience in professional skincare, clinical education, and advanced aesthetic treatments.

Having spent their careers working directly with clients in clinical settings, they saw first-hand the transformative results that professional LED light therapy and advanced skin treatments could deliver. They also saw the barrier: most people simply couldn't afford clinic visits on the frequency needed to maintain results.

Trudermal was their answer to that gap.

Our Mission

"Everyone deserves access to expert-level skincare, every day."

We created Trudermal with one singular objective: to bridge the gap between professional clinic treatments and at-home care. Not to cut corners or compromise on quality — but to genuinely bring clinic-grade technology into the home in a way that is safe, effective, and accessible.

What We Stand For
