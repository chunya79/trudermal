The Science

How the Halo Restores Your Hair

660nm red light is the clinically proven wavelength for hair follicle stimulation. Here's what happens beneath your scalp with every 10-minute session.

Increases Blood Flow

Red light improves microcirculation to the scalp, delivering more oxygen and nutrients directly to follicles.

Activates Dormant Follicles

Light energy stimulates mitochondrial function in follicle cells, reactivating follicles that have slowed or stopped producing hair.

Extends Growth Phase

Prolongs the anagen (growth) phase of the hair cycle, meaning more hairs are growing at any given time.

Strengthens Hair Fibres

Supports the production of stronger, thicker hair strands and reduces excessive shedding over time.

Usage Guide

How to Use the Halo

01
Cleanse & Dry
Ensure hair is clean and completely dry before each session. Do not apply any products to your scalp or hair beforehand.
02
Insert & Attach
Place the LED device inside the hat with the controller positioned at the back. Attach the battery to the magnetic dots on the back of the hat.
03
Activate
Press and hold the controller button to turn on the device. The Halo automatically shuts off after 10 minutes.
04
Follow Up
Apply Regen Hair Growth Serum after treatment for enhanced results. Repeat every other day for 16 weeks.

For best results use consistently every other day for the full 16-week programme. Take before-and-after photos every 4 weeks to track your progress.

Important: Contraindications

Do not use the Trudermal Halo if you:

  • ⚠️ Are pregnant, planning pregnancy, or breastfeeding
  • ⚠️ Have Lupus erythematosus, photosensitive eczema, or Albinism
  • ⚠️ Have any photosensitive disorder or light-induced headaches
  • ⚠️ Have epilepsy or a genetic eye condition
  • ⚠️ Are taking medications that cause photosensitivity (certain antibiotics, chemotherapy, diuretics)
  • ⚠️ Are undergoing active cancer treatment

If you are unsure whether this device is suitable for you, please consult your healthcare provider before use.
