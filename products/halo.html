Halo FAQs

What is the Trudermal Halo?
Trudermal Halo is an advanced LED hair growth cap designed to support scalp health and promote healthier, thicker hair growth using clinically recognised 660nm red light therapy technology.
Who is the Halo for?
It's suitable for both men and women experiencing hair thinning, alopecia, reduced hair density, increased shedding, early signs of hair loss, or general scalp health concerns.
How many LEDs does it have?
The Halo has a total of 180 medical-grade LED bulbs per treatment, providing full coverage of the scalp.
Is it portable?
Yes — the Halo is completely portable and battery-powered, so you can easily move around while wearing it.
Is it safe and approved?
Yes. The Trudermal Halo is FDA & TGA approved as a Class IIa medical device and conforms to international safety standards. It's TGA approved for safety and effectiveness for all skin types.
How often should I use it?
Use for 10 minutes every other day over a 16-week period for optimal hair growth, then maintain as required.

Before You Start

Simple Sensitivity Test

1. Place the device over your inner arm.
2. Run a full 10-minute treatment.
3. Stop immediately if your skin feels hot or uncomfortable.
4. Wait 24 hours to check for any redness or itching.
5. If unsuitable, contact support — returns accepted within 14 days.

Important: Contraindications

Do not use the Trudermal Halo if you:

  • ⚠️ Are pregnant, planning pregnancy, or breastfeeding
  • ⚠️ Have Lupus erythematosus, photosensitive eczema, or Albinism
  • ⚠️ Have any photosensitive disorder or light-induced headaches
  • ⚠️ Have epilepsy or a genetic eye condition
  • ⚠️ Are taking medications that cause photosensitivity (certain antibiotics, chemotherapy, diuretics)
  • ⚠️ Are undergoing active cancer treatment

If you are unsure whether this device is suitable for you, please consult your healthcare provider before use.
