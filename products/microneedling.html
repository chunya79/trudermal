Usage Guide

How to Use the Infusion Kit

01
Cleanse Skin
Thoroughly cleanse your face, neck, or target area. Skin must be clean and free of makeup, SPF, or product.
02
Fill the Device
Fill the device chamber with one serum ampule. Hold upside down for 1 minute to allow serum to saturate the needle head.
03
Stamp in Sections
Start at the centre of the face and work outward with 50% overlap between stamps. Repeat 2–3 passes over each section.
04
Apply & Rest
Apply excess serum to the skin. Avoid makeup and direct sun exposure for 24 hours post-treatment. Use SPF the following morning.

Recommended frequency: every 2 weeks. Visible changes typically appear after 4–6 sessions (10+ weeks). Needle head is single-use only — do not reuse.
