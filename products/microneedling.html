The Technology

How Micro-Infusion Works

A patented 20-point 24k gold spring-loaded stamp creates microscopic pathways in the skin at a 600-micron depth. As it stamps, it simultaneously delivers a peptide-rich serum deep into the skin — boosting absorption by up to 200% and triggering the skin's natural collagen response.

Questions

Microneedling FAQs

What is the Microneedling Infusion Kit?
An at-home micro-infusion system that pairs a 24k gold stamp with a peptide-rich serum to stimulate collagen and dramatically boost serum absorption.
Are the heads reusable?
No — the needle heads are single-use only, for hygiene and best results.
Is it painful or is there downtime?
At a 600-micron depth it's designed to be comfortable with minimal downtime; some light redness can occur and usually settles quickly.
How often should I use it?
Follow the kit's recommended schedule — typically every 1–2 weeks — and always allow skin to recover between sessions.
Can I use my own serums?
The kit is formulated to work with its included peptide serum for safe, predictable results.
Can it be combined with LED light therapy?
Yes — it pairs well with the Glow or Neck LED masks as part of a complete routine.
What's the 90-Day Money-Back Guarantee?
If you're not satisfied, you're covered by a 90-day money-back guarantee — contact support to submit a claim.

Important: Do Not Use If You

  • ⚠️ Have active acne, open wounds, cuts or unhealed skin
  • ⚠️ Have eczema, rosacea, psoriasis, dermatitis or sunburn
  • ⚠️ Have had laser, peels, needling, filler or Botox in the last 2 weeks
  • ⚠️ Are using AHAs/BHAs, retinol or exfoliants (avoid 48 hours before/after)
  • ⚠️ Have a history of keloid scarring or metal allergies
  • ⚠️ Are taking Roaccutane/Accutane (or have within the last 12 months)
  • ⚠️ Are pregnant or breastfeeding (consult your doctor)

If you are unsure whether this kit is suitable for you, please consult your healthcare provider before use.
