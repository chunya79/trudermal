Usage Guide

How to Use the Glow Mask

01
Cleanse
Start with thoroughly cleansed, bare skin. Do not apply serums, creams, or SPF before treatment — these create a barrier that reduces light effectiveness.
02
Select Your Mode
Press and hold the controller button to power on. Cycle to your chosen treatment mode. Use the same mode for 6 full weeks before switching.
03
Treat for 10 Minutes
Wear the mask for the full 10-minute session. The device automatically shuts off. You can move around freely — it's completely portable.
04
Apply Skincare
After treatment, apply your serums and moisturiser. Your skin's absorption is optimised immediately post-treatment.

Recommended frequency: 3–5 sessions per week. Results are typically visible from week 4. Consistency is key — use 3–4 times weekly for a minimum of 6 weeks for best results.

Important: Contraindications

Do not use the Trudermal Glow if you:

  • ⚠️ Are pregnant, planning pregnancy, or breastfeeding
  • ⚠️ Have Lupus erythematosus, photosensitive eczema, or Albinism
  • ⚠️ Have any photosensitive disorder or light-induced headaches
  • ⚠️ Have epilepsy or a genetic eye condition
  • ⚠️ Are taking medications that cause photosensitivity
  • ⚠️ Are currently using prescriptive Retin-A (discontinue 7 days before use)
Sensitivity Test: Before full treatment, place the device over your inner arm and run a full 10-minute session. Wait 24 hours. If no adverse reaction occurs, proceed with facial treatment.
