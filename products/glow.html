Questions

Glow FAQs

What is the Trudermal Glow?
An advanced LED light therapy face mask that uses three clinically chosen wavelengths to treat acne, signs of ageing and overall skin health — at home, in 10-minute sessions.
Who is it for?
Suitable for all skin types and anyone wanting to address acne, fine lines, uneven texture, redness or dullness.
How often should I use it?
Use 3–5 times per week, 10 minutes per session, for the first 4–6 weeks, then maintain as required.
Which mode is best for acne vs anti-ageing?
Use Mode 1 (Anti-Acne) or Mode 2 (Acne Repair) for breakouts, and Mode 3 (Cell Rejuvenator) for fine lines, collagen and radiance.
How many LED bulbs does it have?
132 medical-grade LED bulbs in total, angled for full-face coverage.
Is it safe and approved?
Yes — it's FDA & TGA approved as a Class IIa medical device and is suitable for sensitive skin and all skin types.
Can I travel with it?
Yes — the mask is lightweight and portable, so you can keep up your routine anywhere.

Before You Start

Simple Sensitivity Test

1. Place the device against your inner arm.
2. Run a full 10-minute treatment.
3. Stop immediately if your skin feels hot or uncomfortable.
4. Wait 24 hours to check for any redness or itching before full use.

Important: Contraindications

Do not use the Trudermal Glow if you:

  • ⚠️ Are pregnant, planning pregnancy, or breastfeeding
  • ⚠️ Have Lupus erythematosus, photosensitive eczema, or Albinism
  • ⚠️ Have any photosensitive disorder or light-induced headaches
  • ⚠️ Have epilepsy or a genetic eye condition
  • ⚠️ Are taking medications that cause photosensitivity (certain antibiotics, chemotherapy, diuretics)

If you are unsure whether this device is suitable for you, please consult your healthcare provider before use.
