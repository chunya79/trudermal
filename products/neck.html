The Problem

The Most Neglected Area in Skincare

The neck and décolletage are among the first areas to reveal age — yet most skincare routines stop at the jawline. The skin here is thinner, has fewer oil glands, and receives just as much UV exposure as the face, making it particularly vulnerable to:

  • → Crepey, loose, or thinning skin
  • → Loss of firmness and definition
  • → Chest redness and uneven tone
  • → Horizontal neck lines and wrinkles
  • → Sun damage and pigmentation

Trudermal's neck and décolletage mask was designed specifically to treat this area — with wavelengths calibrated for the unique characteristics of neck and chest skin.
