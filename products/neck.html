The Most Neglected Area in Skincare

Your Neck Reveals Age First

The skin on your neck and décolletage is thinner and has fewer oil glands than your face, so it loses firmness and develops crepey lines sooner. Most skincare routines stop at the jawline — this device extends professional LED therapy to where it's needed most.

Questions

Neck & Décolletage FAQs

How does it work?
It delivers red, near-infrared and deep near-infrared light to the neck and chest, stimulating collagen and supporting cellular repair to firm and smooth the skin.
Who is it for?
Anyone wanting to address crepey skin, loss of firmness or visible ageing on the neck and décolletage — and on the hands.
Why treat the neck separately from the face?
Neck and chest skin is thinner with fewer oil glands, so it ages faster and needs a contoured device that fits the area properly.
How many LEDs does it have?
Up to 132 medical-grade LEDs per treatment across three wavelengths, including the deep 1072nm near-infrared.
Is it safe and approved?
Yes — it's FDA & TGA approved as a Class IIa medical device and conforms to international safety standards.
Can I travel with it?
Yes — it's lightweight and portable for use at home or away.

Important: Contraindications

Do not use the LED Neck & Décolletage Mask if you:

  • ⚠️ Are pregnant, planning pregnancy, or breastfeeding
  • ⚠️ Have Lupus erythematosus, photosensitive eczema, or Albinism
  • ⚠️ Have any photosensitive disorder or light-induced headaches
  • ⚠️ Have epilepsy or a genetic eye condition
  • ⚠️ Are taking medications that cause photosensitivity (certain antibiotics, chemotherapy, diuretics)

If you are unsure whether this device is suitable for you, please consult your healthcare provider before use.
